About Our Tattoo Studio

We are the most underground tattoo studio in Odessa, Ukraine. Our studio has been inking traditional tattoos for over 10 years. Our talented craftsman and merchandise, Ruslan Bauch, is a highly qualified specialist who is not limited to one style. He produces excellent quality work in all styles, which we are proud to showcase.

We use inductive tattoo machines that are rare in the tattoo industry. Our studio also employs talented artists who practice different styles and methods of tattooing. We are committed to providing sincere tattoos for our clients.
